About Apple

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. The company designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Its hardware products include the iPhone smartphone, the iPad tablet computer, the Mac personal computer, the iPod portable media player, and the Apple Watch smartwatch.
